About Me

I'm an AI researcher and engineer dedicated to building trustworthy, interpretable systems for medicine and healthcare. I recently completed my M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence from Amity University, Noida, after a B.Tech in Computer Science from VIT Vellore.

My research sits at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, large language models, and applied AI for clinical decision support, spanning histopathology, radiology, biomedical question answering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems, and intelligent research pipelines. I have contributed to two peer-reviewed conferences (ICAICCIT 2025 and IICTDS 2025), hold an Indian design patent for an AI-driven radiology system, and currently work as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer at Winniio AB (Sweden), developing clinical AI products.

Alongside research, I serve as a technical trainer, mentoring students and early-career engineers on practical machine learning, deep learning, and applied AI workflows. I was recently recognised among the top performers (4th place) at the AI Campus Hackathon organised by Redrob at Amity University, Noida.

On the technical side, I work fluently across the modern AI stack including PyTorch, TensorFlow, Hugging Face, FastAI, OpenCV, FAISS, LangChain, and Docker, and I am just as comfortable wrangling messy clinical datasets as I am fine-tuning models or designing end-to-end systems.

A retrieval-augmented question-answering system for biomedical yes/no questions.
